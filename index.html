What this fixes

Three operational problems that eat into clinic revenue every single week.

Slow enquiry response

A new patient enquires, then books with whoever replies first. If your clinic doesn't respond within the hour—especially outside reception hours—that patient is gone. Automation handles the instant acknowledgement and booking link, even at 10 pm.

DNAs & late cancellations

Did-not-attends and last-minute cancellations leave appointment slots empty with no time to fill them. A structured appointment reminder sequence—sent at the right intervals before the appointment—gives patients enough notice to reschedule while there's still time to fill the gap.

Manual chasing

Reminders, rebooking prompts, discharge follow-ups, review requests—all valuable, all time-consuming to do manually. Your reception time is better spent on patient-facing work. Automation runs these sequences consistently, without anyone having to remember.

The Physio Appointment & Follow‑Up System

A connected set of automated workflows covering the full patient journey—from first enquiry to long-term retention.

1

Enquiry capture → instant reply → booking link

When a new patient contacts your clinic—by web form, email, or social message—they receive an immediate, personalised response acknowledging their enquiry and presenting a direct link to book their first appointment. No waiting until tomorrow morning.

2

Confirmations & reminder sequences

Once an appointment is booked, a confirmation goes out immediately. Automated reminders follow at set intervals (typically 48 hours and 2 hours before the appointment), giving patients the chance to reschedule if they can't attend—rather than simply not showing up.

3

Post-visit follow-up & rebooking prompts

After each session, a short follow-up message checks in with the patient and, where appropriate, prompts them to book their next appointment. This keeps treatment continuity high and reduces the gap between sessions caused by patients forgetting to rebook.

4

Reactivation for dormant patients

Patients who haven't visited in 3, 6, or 12 months receive a friendly, non-intrusive re-engagement message. Many clinics find this alone brings back patients who had simply forgotten to come back—no advertising spend required.

5

Review requests (after positive outcomes)

Where a patient has completed a course of treatment and expressed satisfaction, an automated message invites them to leave a review on your chosen platform. Timing and wording are kept appropriate to a clinical context—no pressure, no medical claims.
